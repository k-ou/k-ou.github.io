The Task

Concept art for a double album vinyl cover for Fall Out Boy's 2013 album, Save Rock and Roll.

My Solution

Inspired by the Young Blood Chronicles, the music videos for Save Rock and Roll, I wanted to make something that was bold, shocking, and gruesome — but also something that was intriguing and beautiful.

Fall Out Boy loves to experiement with their sound, incorporating pop and hip hop and rock and different artists from many different genres, but still maintains a sound that is uniquely their own. As such, early on, I was compelled by the depth that was created in layered paper crafts and felt it appropriately captured Fall Out Boy's many faces.
