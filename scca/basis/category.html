Amusing Ourselves to Death: Public Discourse in the Age of Show Business

Television has conditioned us to tolerate visually entertaining material measured out in spoonfuls of time, to the detriment of rational public discourse and reasoned public affairs. In this eloquent, persuasive book, Neil Postman alerts us to the real and present dangers of this state of affairs, and offers compelling suggestions as to how to withstand the media onslaught. Terms of Services

Basis Magazine serves those who are curious and have an interest in being well-informed, those who are seeking guidance and those who are looking for new and innovative perspectives.

The magazine encourages readers to learn to think for themselves, to look at the world around them with a critical eye, and to move forward with intention. In the face of fear and ignorance, this publication seeks to arm you, the reader, with the knowledge to navigate your life purposefully and with confidence.

To do this, Basis provides its readers with a deeper understanding of the world around them and invites them to simply start asking “Why?”
