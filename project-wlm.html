Wing Luke Museum

The Wing Luke Museum of the Asian American Experience is a museum in Seattle, WA that gives a voice to the local APA community. I supported their marketing team in crafting digital content while they were increasing their online presence during 2020. Here’s a sample of my work with them.

Web Design

Love Letters to the C-ID

#CIDLoveLetters was a campaign asking community members to voice their love for the C-ID. I wrote copy, helped create promo material, aided in art direction, and organized website content (which has since changed).

❮ ❯

Artist Chat

To promote local APA artists during the pandemic, the WLM hosted several live interviews between local APA artists on Instagram Reels.

❮ ❯

Food Tour Bingo

Restaurants in Chinatowns across the U.S. struggled during the COVID-19 pandemic. The WLM and @iheartID created a Bingo giveaway to encourage people to patronize restaurants in the C-ID.

❮ ❯

Shop-o-Rama 2021

During the holiday season each year, the WLM has a Shop-o-Rama, promoting and discounting items in their Marketplace, which is largely comprised of goods by local APA creatives.

❮ ❯

Book-o-Rama 2021

During the holiday season each year, the WLM has a Book-o-Rama, promoting and discounting books from their Marketplace, which largely features local APA authors.
