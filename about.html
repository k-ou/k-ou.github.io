HEY, HELLO.

I’m Kelly Ou.

I’m a graphic designer who finds joy in the little things (e.g. beautiful typography; also, kittens) and peace in the big things (e.g. open, honest communication; also, the ocean). I strive to move through the world each day with authenticity, open-mindedness, kindness, and good coffee.

And, more than anything, I love stories. I especially love to listen to and help others tell their stories, and I appreciate that doing so well means taking on a number of different roles — as a designer and illustrator, as a storyteller and world-builder, as a friend and ally.

So, tell me your story. Let’s see what kind of world we can build.
