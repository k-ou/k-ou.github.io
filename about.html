A snapshot.

I’m a graphic designer who finds joy in the little things (e.g. beautiful typography; also, kittens) and peace in the big things (e.g. open, honest communication; also, the ocean). I strive to move through the world each day with authenticity, open-mindedness, silliness, and good cup of coffee.

Meaningful work, to me, is listening to and helping others tell their stories. And I appreciate that being a truly great storyteller means taking on many different roles — as a designer and illustrator, as a communicator and world-builder, as a friend and ally. This is the kind of craft that I love and aspire to.

So, tell me your story. Let’s see what kind of world we can build.
