WHAT WE MAKE

VENOM

HIGHLIGHT ANOMALIES, TRENDS, AND CONTINUITY

The Visual Explanation and Narration of Models (VENOM) is an extensible data I/O web service framework that enables analyst-in/on-the-loop coordination with a wide-range of automation and automated reasoning analytics.

VENOM visualizes hypotheses-driven evidence based off user and machine learning algorithms to determine probable courses of action.

PLASMA

DATA PREPARATION & PORTFOLIO OPTIMIZATION

PLASMA offers users the ability to create their own analytic workflows regardless of the backend analytic engine. It provides the means to “visually program” custom workflows using modularized components across multiple domains and platforms to include streaming architectures. Users can design, execute, and share their custom analytics in a persistent ecosystem designed to lower the barrier to entry for advanced analytics for the most inexperienced of users.

AutoMLD

AUTOMATED MULTI-LOOK DETECTION

AutoMLD provides a cloud-based software framework to perform automated, machine-aided, persistent monitoring of areas based on a predictive model of previous activity.

DisCorT

DISPLAY OF CORRELATED THREATS

DisCorT is an inline near real-time SIGINT correlator for use on aircraft or other platforms with their own architecture and display capabilities. DisCorT has been nominated as a program of record.

ASA

ANALYST SUPPORT ARCHITECTURE

Providing near real-time multi-INT correlation, analysis, and display toolkit/test bed for National and Tactical information.  ASA leverages advanced correlation algorithms by applying them directly to specific data-types (e.g. COMINT vs. ELINT vs. AIS, etc)  ASA also leverages data modeling and automatic alerting of anomalies and events.
