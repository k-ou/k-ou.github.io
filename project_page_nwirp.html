Know Your Rights Workshop

The Client

The Northwest Immigrant Rights Project is an organization that promotes justice by defending and advancing the rights of immigrants through direct legal services, systemic advocacy, and community education.

The Task

I was tasked with creating a poster for their upcoming "Know Your Rights" Workshops, which educate attendees on their rights. Workshops are held entirely in Spanish.

My Solution

With this poster, I wanted to convey a sense of unity and strength, as well as an immense amount of pride. The workshops are meant to inspire and welcome rather than incite violence or foster unhappiness, so my poster boasts of a community coming together to celebrate, support each other, and give courage to others.

My poster design has been used in flyers and advertisements for future workshops.
