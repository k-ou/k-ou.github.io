ACM-W @ UCLA T-shirt Design

The Client

ACM-W @ UCLA (Association of Computing Machinery - Women) is an organization that supports, celebrates, and advocates for the full engagement of all who are interested in computer science. Additionally, ACM-W endeavors to increase all aspects of diversity in the technical field by providing a range of programs and services to UCLA students.

I am proud to say I played a role in shaping this budding collegiate organization, and I hold it's values and members very close to my heart. So I was thrilled when asked to design a shirt for their members for the upcoming year.
