The Project

Concept landing page to educate general public on the history of Asian Americans and start a conversation on how they are perceived now. Works on desktop only.

The Task

Concept was key. There was so much potential in the topic, so many different aspects of Asian American history and life to explore. Sorting through the plethora of historical information and recent statistics and articles and packaging them into digestible pieces of information was the biggest problem I had.

Solution

The website is modern and journalistic, catching the eye without being flashy. The stark black and white color scheme is punctuated with splashes of warm yellow, tipping a hat to the haters, and a blocky display font is used liberally for dramatic emphasis. Information is summarized and emphasized when needed for easy perusal.
